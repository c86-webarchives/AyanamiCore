      第三届登峰杯赛后总结 / Summary after the third Summit Cup

      9 8月 2018

      添加评论

      首先，老实的承认我的项目逊爆了。

      可能在外人看来，这个项目很有竞争力，但是很大程度上，是我们对项目的包装导致的一种后果，在创新大赛也给我狠狠地捅了一刀：真实性有待论证。

      项目选择与知识量

      一般来说，项目里面有一定数量的信息技术课大纲外知识，有一点选秀内容外知识，再加一到两项绝对要自学的内容（超越竞赛），足矣！多了，反而真实性受到质疑 -> Certain Death。

      很明显的，有大量的参赛者选择进行一种操作叫做“蹭热点”，而所谓的“热点”往往逃不出AI（或者ML）等一些看起来很“黑科技”的东西。或许在真正的营销中这个效果非常不错，但也要考虑到创新竞赛的特殊性。我有幸与好几个选手交流，其中许多都是有大学生（往往还是知名大学大学生）的持续协助，一旦碰到“行内人”，两手抓瞎。

      我必须承认，就算不说，没有哪个创新大赛是不需要独立自主的。而在初赛，判断的唯一依据是论文。

      编写论文

      论文有一定的格式，创新大赛的论文格式似乎并不特别受到老教授的欢迎。

      参阅一些正式的学术期刊可以发现，真正的论文存在特殊的格式，即：概要—研究过程—结论。在实际的竞赛撰写过程中，应该还需要有创新点、日程等必要信息。

      避免大量数据。草草扫过，啊，那里一张表格，那里有一张表格，又有谁会正儿八经去看数据呢？反倒是图片影响力更大一些。这和社交网络有类似之处。用自己过程中的图片（不是摆拍，就算是电脑截图也可以）打动评委，除了要有收集数据的意识来避免最后被迫摆拍，也要注意图片与文字的协调关系。

      活动照片是必须的，但是应当有意识地少放。俗语有云：“做贼心虚”。照片越多，到最后就与在什么地方写（刻）xxx到此一游一样，有刷存在感的嫌疑。如果原来评委觉得没什么问题，这个时候总要打一个问号，思考一下非独立完成的可能性。

      任何科学内容必须查证，反复查证。我们的论文这次出了一个大错，将电池容量写错（14.06写成16.04），幸好没有评委老师看出来，但如果扫一眼发现不对，那就悲惨了。

      标准化论文语言与引用。不可避免的，完成一个项目会需要有大量的参考资料和别人的作品，正如Google学术在主页写的一样，“Stand on the shoulders of giants”。关于这个方面，我个人强烈推荐去参考维基百科的写作规范。（中文版请自备云梯）

      宣传与包装

      适当的包装是需要的。就算项目逊爆了（就像我的），也要想办法让它看起来高大上一点。在登峰杯上看到的的常用伎俩是突出创新点，申请专利和（/或）著作权登记，掩盖短板，强烈的自动化以及二维码。在条件允许的情况下，也可以成品化（我的项目）。

      有的时候，会发现项目太唬人，以至于没人相信项目的真实性，就算项目是真实的。那么这个时候就多拉一个口才好的，而且报名时还不是文科的一起收集更高荣誉。

      辩论与展示

      展示是一门艺术，辩论是一门技术。好的展示可以一下抓着评委的兴趣点，但切记要注意正确地展示，抵制所有科学性错误，有错误切记不可将错就错。

      好的PPT也是成功展示的一个重要辅助工具。PPT可以代替我们做一些我们做不了的事情，例如强调重点，显示图表，放映照片等等。但人还是核心，PPT内容远多于人讲，或者人讲即PPT，都是极端不可取的。然而一些重要性为低的内容（比如研究计划）可以在PPT上打全，但是只讲一两句。

      提前了解老师答辩风格是有益的。稍微早到一点，现场工作人员会叫你晚点来。如果你觉得可以等待，而且不断询问，他们会感受到你对于竞赛的重视，然后就可以询问那几个评委老师的答辩风格了。有些评委可能比较mean，另外一些就比较normal，当然还有很friendly会把答辩引导进discussion的。不同的老师风格，就会为答辩可能碰到的情形做预备。

      答辩不要反驳评委观点。评委知道的比我们多，就算专业不对口，也要让评委讲完。意见就是意见，记录下来，改不改是另外的事情了。

      为了保证答辩顺利流畅，应该全程参与项目，或向合作方获得尽可能多的信息。

      It may be outsiders’ view that this project is very competitive, but to a large extent, it is a consequence of our packaging of the project, and it also gave me a slap in the innovation contest: authenticity remains to be seen Argument.

      Project selection and knowledge

      Generally speaking, there is a certain amount of knowledge outside the outline of the information technology course in the project. There is a little knowledge of the content of the draft, plus one or two items that are absolutely self-study (beyond the competition), enough! More, but the authenticity is questioned -> Certain Death.

      Obviously, there are a large number of contestants who choose to perform an operation called “hot spots”, and the so-called “hot spots” often cannot escape some things that look like “black technology” such as AI (or ML). Perhaps this is very good in real marketing, but it also takes into account the special nature of the innovation competition. I have the privilege of interacting with several players, many of whom are continuing assistance from college students (often well-known university students). Once they encounter “in-house people”, they are both caught.

      I must admit that even if I don’t say, no innovation competition is not independent. In the preliminary round, the only basis for judgment is the paper.

      Write a paper

      The paper has a certain format, and the format of the paper in the innovation competition does not seem to be particularly popular with the old professor.

      See some formal academic journals to find out that real papers exist in a special format, namely: summary—the research process—conclusion. In the actual competition writing process, you should also need the necessary information such as innovation points and schedules.

      Avoid large amounts of data. Scribbled, ah, there is a form there, there is a form, and who will go to see the data? On the contrary, the image is more influential. This is similar to social networking. Use the pictures in your own process (not posing, even computer screenshots) to impress the judges, in addition to the awareness of collecting data to avoid the last forced to shoot, but also pay attention to the coordination relationship between pictures and text.

      A photo of the event is a must, but it should be consciously placed less. There is a saying in the saying: “Be a guilty conscience.” The more photos, the end is the same as where to write (engraved) xxx to this tour, there is a suspicion of brush presence. If the judges feel that there is no problem, this time always ask a question mark and think about the possibility of non-independent completion.

      Any scientific content must be verified and verified. Our paper made a big mistake this time, and the battery capacity was wrong (14.06 was written as 16.04). Fortunately, no judges saw it, but if it was found to be wrong, it would be miserable.

      Standardized papers and citations. Inevitably, completing a project will require a lot of reference material and other people’s work, as Google Scholar wrote on the homepage, “Stand on the shoulders of giants.” In this regard, I personally strongly recommend referring to Wikipedia’s writing specifications.

      Publicity and Packaging

      Appropriate packaging is needed. Even if the project is inferior (like mine), I have to find a way to make it look taller. The common tricks seen on the Aspen Cup are to highlight innovations, apply for patents and/or copyright registrations, cover up short boards, strong automation and QR codes. It can also be finished (my project) if conditions permit.

      Sometimes, the project will be so horrible that no one believes the authenticity of the project, even if the project is real. Then, at this time, it’s better to take a slogan, and it’s not a liberal arts collection to collect higher honors.

      Debate and Presentation

      Showing is an art, and debate is a technology. A good show can grab the judges’ points of interest, but remember to pay attention to the correct display, resist all scientific errors, and remember that you can’t make mistakes.

      A good PPT is also an important aid to successful presentations. PPT can do something for us that we can’t do, such as emphasizing emphasis, displaying charts, showing photos, and more. But people are still the core, PPT content is far more than people speak, or people say PPT, are extremely undesirable. However, some low-importance content (such as research plans) can be used on the PPT, but only one or two sentences.

      It’s helpful to know the teacher’s defense style in advance. A little earlier, the field staff will ask you to come later. If you feel that you can wait and keep asking, they will feel your attention to the competition, and then you can ask the judges’ styles of the judges. Some judges may compare mean, others are more normal, and of course, very friendly will lead the defense into the discussion. Different teacher styles will prepare for the situation that the defense may encounter.

      The defense should not refute the judges’ views. The judges know more than us, even if the profession is not right, let the judges finish. The opinions are opinions, and it is another matter to record them.

      In order to ensure the smooth and smooth response, you should participate in the project all the time, or get as much information as possible from the partner.
